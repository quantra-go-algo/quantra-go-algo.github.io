    How do I get a certificate for completing a course?¶

    You will get a certificate for each course separately within a few hours of completion of the course. The certificates are downloadable from your account tab My Certificates.

    The link to the My Certificates tab is available here.

    How do I access my course certificate?¶

    You can download your certificate by clicking on the profile icon at the top right corner of the page and selecting `Certificates' from the menu (reference image added below).

    Upon entering the certificates section, you can simply follow the steps below:

    1. Click on the View button to open any of your individual certificate(s)
    2. Click on the Download button
    3. Click on the downloaded PDF file from the downloads section to access the file

    Additional Information

    If you're still unable to see your certificate, simply reach out to us through the Contact Us Page or email us at quantra@quantinsti.com. We will make sure that we help you in resolving your issues at the earliest.

    What are course reviews? How do I review a course?¶

    Once you have completed a course, you will be given the choice to share your review. This would help Quantra in enhancing the course and will also assist future students to understand more about the course from a fellow student’s perspective.

    To give a review, you can follow the steps below:

    1. Complete the course you have enrolled in
    2. Click on the Thumbs Up icon on the header of the learning portal
    3. Once you have drafted the review, click on the Submit button to share the review with us

    Note

    In case you're unable to see the Thumbs Up icon, it might be possible that you have not completed the course yet.

    However, if you're sure that you've completed the course and still can't submit your review, then please feel free to reach out to our team at quantra@quantinsti.com. We will make sure that we help you at the earliest.
