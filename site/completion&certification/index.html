    How do I get a certificate for completing a course?¶

    You will get a certificate for each course separately within a few hours of completion of the course. The certificates are downloadable from your account tab My Certificates.

    The link to the My Certificates tab is available here.

    How do I access my course certificate?¶

    You can download your certificate by clicking on the profile icon at the top right corner of the page and selecting `Certificates' from the menu.

    Upon entering the certificates section, you can simply follow the steps below:

    1. Click on the View button to open any of your individual certificate(s)
    2. Click on the Download button
    3. Click on the downloaded PDF file
